WeaponDescriptiveName=SAET-60 Torpedo
WeaponDescription=Special: Anti-ship only.\nThe SAET-60M is an anti-ship torpedo specifically designed for the self-defense from ASW assets. It can lock only on surface targets and useless against submerged submarines. Due to it passive sensor system and the anti-ship only usage, its effective sensor range is four times smaller than specified and is about 600m (656 yards).

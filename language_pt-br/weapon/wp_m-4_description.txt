WeaponDescriptiveName=SA-N-4 Gecko (M-4 "Оса-МА") SAM
WeaponDescription=Special: Universal\nThe Osa-MA(NATO reporting name: SA-N-4 Gecko) is a Soviet short-range, surface-to-air missile system for shipboard point defense. It is a naval variant of the SA-8 Gecko system. Guidance system is universal and allows switch between missile, aircraft and ship-attack mode. Mode can be switched in the weapon settings just before launch.

WeaponDescriptiveName=Missile de Croisière Naval (MdCN)
WeaponDescription=This is the first cruise missile developped for french navy to be fired from a fregate or submarine , the first firing test from a submarine (from Suffren clas SSN) was performed in 20th of October 2020,  the missile is housed inside a watertight launched capsule (VSM), which is fired by the submarine's torpedo-launch tubes. On leaving the water, the capsule is ejected and the missile's motor is ignited.

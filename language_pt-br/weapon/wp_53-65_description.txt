WeaponDescription=Special: Wake-homing\nThe 53-65 torpedo family are Russian made wake-homing torpedoes designed to destroy surface ships. Wake-homing torpedoes should be aimed to cross wake behind ship's stern. Surfaced submarines can also be hit with WH torpedoes.

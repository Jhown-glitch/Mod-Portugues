WeaponDescriptiveName=SS-N-3A Shaddock (P-6) Missile
WeaponDescription=Special: Universal\nThe SS-N-3A Shaddock was a anti-ship/land-attack missile carried by The Juliett and Echo II guided missile submarines. Guidance system is universal and allows switch between ship-attack and land-attack mode. Mode can be switched in the weapon settings just before launch.

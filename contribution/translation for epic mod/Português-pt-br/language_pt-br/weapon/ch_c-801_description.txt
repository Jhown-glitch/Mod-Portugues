WeaponDescriptiveName=YJ-8 Anti-Ship Missile
WeaponDescription=YJ-8 anti-ship missile(NATO reporting name: CSS-N-4 Sardine) is very similar to the French Exocet and US Harpoon missiles. From submarines it is launched on using a canister similar to Sub-Harpoon, and features similar performance to its western counterparts. Its warhead power is often insufficient to sink a modern warship in one hit.

2002:\nTaking advantage of the deplorable state of the Russian Armed Forces, NATO forces decided to deliver a decisive crushing blow to Russia and the countries supporting it in the UN Security Council. The first ground battle involving armored vehicles, artillery and aviation took place in the CIS (Commonwealth of Independent States) countries. The war between NATO forces and Russian forces was officially declared. US armed forces were fully mobilized, meanwhile the convoy of its miliatry personnels, resources, and weapons cross the Atlantic have already begun. Because of being weakened by the civil war in the Caucasus and the deplorable state of the economy, what Russia can rely on are only the remaining military-industrial complex of the former USSR and the strength in the spirit of its people.
